## Histology of Malignant Thyroid Tumours (5)
1. Papillary: least aggressive with lymph node spread
2. Follicular: moderately aggressive with vascular spread
3. Anaplastic: aggressive with very poor prognosis
4. Medullary: from calcitonin-secreting parafollicular C cells
5. Lymphoma: particularly in Hashimoto's thyroiditis
